Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book for a wedding dress?

We recommend booking at least 6-9 months before your wedding to allow sufficient time for design, creation, and fittings. However, we also offer rush services for shorter timelines.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for clients who cannot visit our boutique in person. These sessions include design discussions, fabric selection, and measurements guidance.

What countries do you deliver to?

We deliver worldwide to virtually any country. Our emergency delivery service ensures your fashion needs are met no matter where you're located.

How does the emergency stitching service work?

If you have a fashion emergency anywhere in the world, contact us and we'll connect you with the nearest qualified tailor from our network who can assist with repairs or alterations.

Can I modify a design from your gallery?

Absolutely! We specialize in customizations. You can select any design from our gallery as a starting point, and we'll modify it to your specific preferences and measurements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and payment platforms. For custom orders, we typically require a 50% deposit with the balance due upon completion.
